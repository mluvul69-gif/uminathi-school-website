Our History

uMinathi Christian School was established with the vision of providing quality Christian-based education to learners within the local community. The school was founded in response to the need for accessible secondary education for learners who were previously required to travel long distances to attend school.

In its early years, uMinathi Christian School operated from rented facilities while laying the foundation for future growth. Through the support of community leaders, donors, and philanthropic organisations, the school was later able to move into its own purpose-built premises.

The development of the school was made possible through partnerships with charitable foundations and the generosity of the local traditional authority, who donated land to support the project. The completed facilities provided classrooms designed to create a safe and supportive learning environment.

Beginning with lower secondary grades, the school gradually expanded its academic offerings as learner enrolment increased. uMinathi Christian School continues to grow with a commitment to academic excellence, discipline, moral values, and service to the community.
